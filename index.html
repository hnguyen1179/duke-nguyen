Recent Work

Cellular Automata: a study on complex systems

Journey to the edge of chaos as we explore the concept of complex systems. This project, which utilizes plain JS and the d3 library, is a visual journal detailing the duality of how simple these systems can be on first glance and how complex they can become on further inspection.

Chimp Casino

A fun project that implements a MERN stack. In this project, myself and a group of 3 other friends created an online multiplayer game where you play as a chimp wondering around a casino floor. In it, you're able to play card games such as poker and blackjack along with slots with other chimp players.
